Our Story

Where it all began

Snuggle & Stroll started with one simple belief: pets deserve care that feels like home. No kennels. No stress. No cold, unfamiliar environments. Just warmth, routine, and genuine companionship.

As a lifelong dog owner, I always struggled to find care that felt personal and trustworthy. So I built the service I wished existed — one where pets stay in their own space, with sitters who treat them like family.

Our mission

We’re here to make pet care feel effortless, safe, and heart‑centered. Every sitter in our network is vetted, trained, and chosen for their compassion as much as their experience.

  • In‑home visits that reduce stress
  • Walks tailored to your pet’s pace and personality
  • Real‑time updates and photos
  • Custom routines for feeding, meds, and comfort

What makes us different

Snuggle & Stroll isn’t a gig app — it’s a curated network of real pet lovers who are trained, background‑checked, and supported by a hands‑on team. We focus on quality, consistency, and building long‑term relationships with families.

  • We prioritize emotional comfort and routine
  • We match pets with sitters based on personality and needs
  • We offer flexible scheduling and transparent communication
  • We treat every pet like a member of our own family

Meet the founder

Hi, I’m Ashleigh — lifelong dog mom, animal‑behavior nerd, and the heart behind Snuggle & Stroll. I created this service because I believe pets deserve care that feels personal, calm, and deeply attentive. Every detail of Snuggle & Stroll is designed with that philosophy in mind.

When I’m not working with pets, you’ll find me studying animal behavior, exploring new trails, or curled up with my own pup on the balcony.

Our values

  • Compassion: Every pet is treated with patience, warmth, and respect.
  • Trust: We communicate clearly and consistently with pet parents.
  • Safety: Sitters are trained, vetted, and supported by our team.
  • Joy: We believe pet care should feel happy — for pets and humans alike.

How we choose our sitters

Every sitter in our network goes through a careful selection process. We look for people who are not only experienced with animals, but who genuinely love what they do.

  • Background checks and identity verification
  • Hands‑on experience with dogs, cats, or small animals
  • Training in safety, routines, and communication
  • Commitment to compassionate, respectful care

Looking ahead

Snuggle & Stroll is growing — and so is our vision. We’re building tools that make scheduling easier, communication smoother, and care more personalized than ever.

Whether you're booking a walk, planning a trip, or just need someone to check in on your furry friend, we’re here to make the process seamless and stress‑free.
