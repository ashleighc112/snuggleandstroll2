About SAS

As a lifelong pet owner, I always struggled leaving my pet with a stranger — not knowing their personality, background, or how they would truly treat my dog when I wasn’t there.

So I built the solution I wished existed. Snuggle & Stroll connects loving pet parents with trusted, background-checked sitters whose personalities, experience, and specialties are clearly matched to your pet’s needs.

This isn’t kennel care. This is comfort, familiarity, and heart-led in-home support.
